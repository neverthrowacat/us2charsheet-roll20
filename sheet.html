Trauma


Your sense of self has been shattered by your death, leaving you traumatized. You begin each session with at least 2-trauma marked, but can clear itand any additional trauma you take via trauma moves. If you ever fill your trauma track, the MC may call for you to make a trauma move at any time, but you may always choose which trauma move you make in the moment.
The first time you take harm in a scene, mark trauma; when you fill up on harm, your corpus is scattered. Mark trauma to reform in a few days at one of your anchors, or mark 3-trauma to reform immediately at an anchor of the MCs choice. If you cannot mark trauma while destroyed, the MC decides how/when you reform.
